# Moderation rules

> This is the initial policy inferred from the project specification. It will be refined
> incrementally as real moderation cases reveal gaps or ambiguity.

## 1. No personal attacks

Do not insult, demean, ridicule, threaten, or attack a person or group. Criticism of a
person's specific conduct, claims, or work is allowed when expressed without abuse.

## 2. Do not present unsupported accusations as fact

Do not state or strongly imply that a person committed wrongdoing when the message does
not establish a reliable basis for that claim. Clearly framed questions, opinions,
uncertainty, requests for evidence, and good-faith discussion of documented facts are
allowed.

## Interpretation

Moderate conservatively. Context, quotation, counterspeech, self-reference, fiction,
and good-faith discussion of prohibited language are not violations merely because they
contain matching words. Block only clear violations, not ambiguous or borderline text.
